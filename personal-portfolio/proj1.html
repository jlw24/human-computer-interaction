Wireframing and Prototyping

This app allows users to maintain "bookshelves" of books that they want to read, are reading now, and that they've finished reading.

This mockup supports all of the following 6 tasks:

  • User searches for a favorite author or book title and adds a new book to their list of things to read.
  • User notices a currently trending book and adds it to their list of things to read.
  • User removes a book from the list of things they are currently reading because they didn't like the first chapter.
  • User moves a book from the currently reading shelf to the finished reading shelf.
  • User selects a book to start reading from their list of things to read.
  • User rates a book that they just finished reading and writes a short review of the book. They want to strongly recommend it to others.
